Date #1: Walk in the Arb & Condados

Abby helped me cut my hair an hour before you came over while I panicked. You showed up with flowers and it made me so happy. Abby was hiding in the bushes not so discreetly. You asked me all about running and you told me all about your grandparents' Swiss model house in the backyard. You had done your research before and found out Condados was my favorite restaurant. Date #2: Ticket to Ride & Adoration

I absolutely crushed you at the game (obviously cause I never lose at anything). I asked you after adoration what we would do about me moving to Tennessee. You told me you didn't know but we would figure it out.

Walk to 8pm Mass Together

You asked me to walk to Mass together and I was so excited. I hugged you for the first time during the sign of peace. Mg was so mad at me because I left her stranded on the end by turning to you first.

Date #3: Picnic

This date is when I knew things were going to be so good. I had so much fun asking you questions. My favorite one was at the end I read the card "Are you pursuing any new relationships right now?" and you said "Just with you" and I had so many butterflies in my stomach. You also got absolutely fried.

April 30!!!

I thought you were going to ask me and make it official at breakfast but that did not happen. But YAY we got to hang out again that night. All your roomates were downstairs cussing up a storm playing the wii and Ryan kept walking past giggiling but avoiding eye contact. Then finally you said "I have no idea what I'm doing, but should we make it official". After I said yes, I made you happy dance with me woo!

MEGA speed run

You met my whole family like two days later, we held hands and kissed for the first time, Honduras/Utah, and then about two days together in AA before we both went home. Shoutout meeting your dad and you being silent.

I wouldn't change a single thing. I love you, Liam.
